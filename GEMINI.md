of this project, and the first three (TypeScript/Excalibur, AffineScript/PixiJS)
are **dead**. The live codebase — this one — is **Rust and Elixir**. There is no
browser, no Canvas, no `requestAnimationFrame`, no npm. `AGENTS.md` covers this
and the other traps in full.
